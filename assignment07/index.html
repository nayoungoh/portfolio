Final Project Proposal

Topics

My website is going to be about helping people who are planning to or are interested in visiting Seoul, South Korea. People are increasingly drawn to visit Seoul, South Korea, for its dynamic blend of tradition and modernity. Tourists are captivated by the city's vibrant street food scene, futuristic architecture, bustling shopping districts, and not to mention the K-pop phenomenon happening globally. I would love to make a website dedicated to showing my hometown and encouraging many tourists to visit!

Audience

The audience will be non-Korean foreigners who are planning to or interested in visiting Korea, especially the capital, Seoul.

Design & Style

The design will be kept simple for a clean look since I will put a lot of words for descriptions, making the page readable. But I’ll also be adding a pop of colors and images to keep the website interesting.

Scope

The website will have 3 pages. A homepage with an image and introduction of Seoul, a recommendations page with a drop-down menu including “Eat and Drink” section, “Places” section and “Tips” section, and finally an inquiry page.
