Kiss of Life (Korean: 키스 오브 라이프), also known as KIOF, is a South Korean girl group formed by S2 Entertainment in 2023.
The group consists of four members: Julie (top-right), Natty (top-left), Belle (bottom-right), and Haneul (bottom-left). They debuted on July 5, 2023, with the extended play (EP) Kiss of Life.
Their 1st Single Album [Midas Touch] will be released on April 3, 2024 6pm KST.
